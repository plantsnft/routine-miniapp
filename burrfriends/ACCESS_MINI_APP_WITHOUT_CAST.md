## ‚úÖ Method 5: Add to Home Screen (iOS)

1. **Open your Vercel URL in Safari** (iPhone)
2. **Tap the Share button**
3. **Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"**
4. **Name it** (e.g., "Poker Mini App")
5. **Tap "Add"**
6. **Now you have an icon on your home screen!**
7. **When you tap it, it should open in Warpcast** (if configured)

## üÜò If It Doesn't Work

### "URL doesn't open as mini app"
- Make sure you're using **Warpcast app** (not browser)
- Try the **search bar method** first
- Check that your deployment is live

### "Can't find search in Warpcast"
- Look for the **magnifying glass icon** at the top
- Or tap the **compose button** and paste URL there
- Then tap the link

### "Still opens in browser"
- Make sure you have **Warpcast app installed**
- Try opening Warpcast first, then pasting URL in search
- Some URLs need to be accessed via cast initially
